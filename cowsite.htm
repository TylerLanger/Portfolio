Cows are pretty great!

Have you ever wondered how important cows are to humanity? Just think about it for a second, and you can quickly realise how incredible these members of the bovine family really are. They're essentially giant blocks of meat that lumber around adhering to our imposed will, while at the same time producing nutritious milk that they will happily surrender to us. Humans have exploited these qualities to such a degree that we now entirely depend on cows for our survival; they provide millions of pounds of meat for us each year, and just think of all the food products today that contain milk. Do you like ice cream? Thank cows for that. Cheese? Cows are also responsible for that. Yoghurt? Cows. Cows are even used to produce huge percentages of the world's leather. It is my personal opinion is that cows are criminally underrated in the western world, and deserve to be raised to a higher station of recognition.
